The Citijan Seva Portal,implemented on the UPYOG platform, offers several benefits to municipalities and citizens:

Unified Access

Provides a single, integrated platform for accessing a wide range of municipal services, making it easier for citizens to interact with their local goverment.

Streamlined Processes

Simplifies administrative procedures by centralizing various services like NOCs, licenses, and permissions, reducing the time and effort required for approvals.

Enhanced Transparency

Facilitates greater transparency and accountability by offering real-time tracking of services requests, applications, and approvals.

Improved Efficiency

Reduces duplication of effort across different municipalities by standardizing processes and data management, leading to more efficient service delivery.

Citizen Empowerment

Empowers citizens by providing 24/7 access to services and information, allowing them to engage with municipal services at their convenience.
